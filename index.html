Our Community Organisations

NEXT TIME YOU SHOP — Ask Yourself: WHY DOES IT MATTER?

Because every dollar spent locally strengthens our community. It supports families, creates jobs, and lightens the burden on vital organisations like Staten Island Bikur Cholim and COJO, which provide discreet, life-changing support to those in crisis.

Bikur Cholim assists with medical equipment, hospital visits, meals, transportation, and emergency aid. COJO offers case management, benefits assistance, youth and senior programming, and has operated Staten Island's largest kosher food pantry since 1980.

Have Maaser to give? Start at home. Your first stop should always be your own community.
