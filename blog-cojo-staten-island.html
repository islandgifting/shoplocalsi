COJO Staten Island: Serving Our Community Since 1968

July 2026 Community News 5 min read

For more than half a century, COJO Staten Island has been a cornerstone of the Jewish community, providing hope, guidance, and support to families throughout every stage of life. Since 1968, the COJO Pantry has faithfully served the Jewish community of Staten Island, ensuring that no family has to face hardship alone.

Pre-Pesach Food Distribution

One of the most inspiring examples of COJO's dedication is its annual Pre-Pesach Food Distribution. Each year, in partnership with every kehilla across Staten Island, COJO works tirelessly to ensure that families can celebrate Yom Tov with dignity. Hundreds of families receive food and essential Pesach items, allowing them to focus on the joy and meaning of the holiday instead of worrying about how they will put food on the table.

"When you reach out to COJO Staten Island, you will be welcomed with compassion and respect — no matter where you come from or which shul you attend."

Housing & Eviction Prevention

But COJO's work extends far beyond food assistance. When families face the possibility of losing their homes, COJO steps in. Through tireless advocacy and guidance, COJO has achieved remarkable success in helping residents secure affordable housing, prevent evictions, and stop foreclosures. For countless Staten Island families, what seemed like an impossible situation became a new beginning because someone at COJO was willing to fight for them.

Utility Assistance

When the lights are about to be shut off or a family receives a utility termination notice, COJO is often the first call they make. The organization works diligently with utility companies and assistance programs to resolve shutoffs and restore essential services, bringing stability back to households during difficult times.

Connecting Seniors to the Right Resources

COJO is also deeply committed to our seniors. Navigating available services can be overwhelming, and COJO helps by referring elderly residents and their families to homecare services, food programs, and other support resources — making sure no senior falls through the cracks simply because they didn't know where to turn.

A Lifeline for Every Family

Perhaps what makes COJO truly special is that it is there for families when they have nowhere else to turn. When a large family is struggling financially, facing an unexpected crisis, or simply doesn't know where to begin, COJO Staten Island is often the number they call. Every situation is treated with compassion, confidentiality, and genuine care.

Leadership

Leading this incredible organization is Rabbi Mendy Mirocznik, a respected and familiar figure throughout Staten Island. For many years, Rabbi Mirocznik has devoted himself to strengthening the community, advocating for families, and ensuring that every individual is treated with dignity and respect. His leadership reflects COJO's mission of serving every member of the community with kindness and dedication.
