Why Every Dollar You Spend Locally Makes a Difference

July 2025 Community News 3 min read

Every time you choose to spend your money at a local business on Staten Island, you are doing something far greater than simply making a purchase. You are investing in your neighbor, your community, and the future of the place we all call home.

Money That Stays Here, Works Here

When you shop local, your dollars don't disappear into a corporate headquarters hundreds of miles away. They circulate right here — paying the salary of a local employee, covering the rent of a family-owned storefront, and funding the small business owner who coaches your child's Little League team or sits next to you in shul. A thriving local economy means families earning a living, paying their bills on time, and standing on their own two feet with dignity.

"When our community shops local, we ease the pressure off COJO and Bikur Cholim — and we allow our neighbors to pay their bills with dignity."

Easing the Burden on Those Who Help the Most

Organizations like COJO Staten Island and Bikur Cholim are cornerstones of our community — providing food, housing assistance, utility help, medical support, and so much more to families in need. But here is a truth we don't always think about: the stronger our local economy, the fewer families need to make that call. When local businesses thrive, families earn more, spend less in crisis, and rely less on emergency support. Shopping local is one of the most powerful and quiet ways to contribute to the same mission that COJO and Bikur Cholim carry every single day.

We Cry When a Store Closes

We have all experienced it. A beloved local shop — the kind of place where the owner knows your name, where you could run an account, where the service was personal and the quality was real — quietly closes its doors. And it is only then, standing in front of the darkened window, that we realize how much that place meant to us. The truth is, we should not have to wait for that moment. Every time we choose convenience over community, every time we order from a faceless website instead of calling the local shop down the block, we chip away at something irreplaceable.

"We cry when a store closes down — and it is only then that we realize how essential it was."

If We All Do Our Part, We Can Thrive Together

The good news is that the solution is entirely in our hands. It does not require a donation. It does not require a meeting or a committee or a fundraiser. It simply requires a choice — made every day, by every one of us — to think local first. If every family on Staten Island made even a small shift toward supporting local businesses, the ripple effect would be enormous. New businesses would be encouraged to open here. Existing businesses would grow, hire more, and give back more. Our streets would be more vibrant. Our community organizations would be less strained. And our neighbors would be more secure.

ShopLocalSI.com exists for exactly this reason — to make it as easy as possible for you to find the local businesses, services, and talent that our community has to offer. Every listing on this site is a real family, a real dream, a real contribution to the fabric of Staten Island Jewish life.
