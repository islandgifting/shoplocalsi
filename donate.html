Where Your Donation Goes

Every dollar you give to COJO Staten Island goes directly to supporting Jewish families across Staten Island. COJO is there when families have nowhere else to turn — treating every situation with compassion, confidentiality, and genuine care.

  • Food Pantry & Pesach Distribution — Hundreds of families receive food and Yom Tov essentials each year, allowing them to celebrate with dignity.
  • Housing & Eviction Prevention — COJO advocates tirelessly to keep families in their homes and stop foreclosures before they happen.
  • Utility Assistance — When the lights are about to go out, COJO is often the first call a family makes.
  • Senior Referrals — Connecting seniors and their families to homecare services, food programs, and the support resources they need.
  • Crisis Support — When an unexpected emergency strikes, COJO is there — no questions asked, no judgment, just help.

Your donation is fully tax-deductible. Donations are processed by Igud Harabonim of America Inc, a 501(c)(3) nonprofit recognized by the IRS since 1981 (EIN: 11-2575788), and are designated for COJO Staten Island programs. You will receive an official tax receipt for your records.

Important: When completing your donation, please add a note designating your gift "For COJO Programs" so it is directed to COJO Staten Island's community work.
