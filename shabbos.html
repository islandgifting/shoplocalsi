Motzei Shabbos

The Three Shitos Explained

Shabbos ends at nightfall — tzeis hakochavim (“the emergence of the stars”). Communities follow different opinions on exactly when that moment is. Here's what each shitah represents:

Earliest · ~40 min
R. Tukachinsky Opinion
Approximately 40 minutes after shkia. Followed in Eretz Yisrael and by many Sefardim.
3 Medium Stars · ~50 min
Common in the US
Approximately 50 minutes after shkia, when three medium-sized stars are visible. The most widely accepted zman in Ashkenazi communities.
Latest · 72 min
Rabbeinu Tam
72 minutes after shkia. Followed by many chassidishe communities and used as a chumra by others.
